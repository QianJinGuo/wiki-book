## Webinar On Demand The AI access shift: Securing agentic and non-human identities
AI agents and automation are reshaping how access works. Increasingly, credentials are created and used by machine workflows on developer endpoints, inside IDEs, and across CI/CD pipelines — outside the visibility and control of traditional IAM.
As non-human identities multiply, security teams face growing governance blind spots, credential sprawl, and weak auditability. We can’t turn back the clock on AI agents, so adapting to these new challenges is an urgent priority.
In this session, we’ll explore how to discover, secure, and audit non-human access starting at the endpoint, so organizations can adopt AI and automation without expanding unmanaged credential risk.
Key takeaways:
*   Why AI agents and machine identities are expanding access risk
*   Where non-human credential blind spots emerge
*   How to secure credentials at time of use instead of hardcoding them
*   How to generate clear audit trails across humans and machines
