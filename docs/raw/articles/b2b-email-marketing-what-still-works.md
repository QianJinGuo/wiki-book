Email is, in my humble opinion, still the greatest marketing channel of all time. It's the only way you can truly own your audience and get a direct line to your buyer.
But the strategy is always changing. What subject lines are getting opened? How do you personalize without sounding like a robot? What is Google doing with the inbox these days? What time should you hit send? What about AI? What role does AI play in the future of marketing emails? Should I doing more? Less? Something different with our email strategy?
The best thing to do is to get a few marketers on the horn and share what they are actually doing, right now in Q2 2026.
So for this Exit Five Live session,we're bringing together three marketers who are running email at their org asking them to show the actual campaigns, copy, workflows, and tools they're using to drive pipeline and get results right now.
We’ll have four marketers on stage, each with five minutes to share something that have learned and done with email marketing right now. You can expect to get information like:
1.   How high-performing teams are producing great emails (they’ll show you the workflow, the tools, and the result)
2.   Examples of great emails that are converting, with takeaways you can apply to your own work
3.   How to measure email and report the impact to leadership
4.   Plus taking all of your questions about email and what’s going on inside of your company
