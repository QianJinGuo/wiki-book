# Anthropic puts Claude agents on a meter across its subscriptions
Anthropic is introducing usage-based metering for Claude AI agents across its subscription tiers, a significant shift in how AI agent services are priced and delivered. This move reflects the growing sophistication of AI agents and the need for more nuanced billing models.
Key developments:
1. **Agent Usage Tracking**: Unlike traditional API pricing based on tokens alone, agent-based pricing can track the number of actions, tasks completed, and the complexity of operations performed by AI agents.
2. **Subscription Tiers**: The new pricing structure includes multiple subscription tiers with different agent capability limits, allowing enterprises to choose plans that match their usage patterns.
3. **Cost Predictability**: While usage-based billing introduces variable costs, the subscription model provides a baseline predictability that pure usage-based models lack.
4. **Enterprise Focus**: The article suggests this pricing model is designed primarily for enterprise customers who want the benefits of AI agents without the unpredictability of pure token-based pricing.
5. **Market Implications**: This pricing innovation could set a new standard for how AI agent services are commercialized, potentially influencing competitors like OpenAI and Google.
The shift to agent-aware pricing reflects the maturation of AI from simple text generation to complex, multi-step task execution.
