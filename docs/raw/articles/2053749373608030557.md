 Lots of companies in RWAs are building absolute monsters. Multiple lines of products, proper infrastructure & onboarding proper users. 
 But if we’re being honest, quite a few of the tokens themselves have struggled a bit.
 At the same time though, we’ve seen a handful of RWA tokens absolutely fly. And I think it’s becoming pretty obvious why.
 People want exposure to the growth of tokenization itself. They want a piece of the companies building the rails for where finance is heading.
 Whether it ends up being a fee switch, revenue share, buybacks, governance value or something else entirely doesn’t even seem to matter right now.
 The market is starting to treat some of these tokens more like early bets on future financial infrastructure rather than just “utility tokens”.
 Everyone wants ownership, or at least exposure, to the best tokenization companies.
