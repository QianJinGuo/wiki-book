FAQ
## Questions, answered.
Get quick answers about pricing, privacy, browser extensions, supported media, and how Reference works across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
Is Reference a subscription or a one-time purchase?
Reference is a one-time purchase and currently costs 4.99 dollars, depending on your App Store region. That single purchase gives you Reference across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
Is Reference private?
Yes. We do not track you, lock you in, or sell your data. Your references stay private, we do not know how you use the app, and your inspiration syncs through iCloud across your own devices in the Apple ecosystem.
Does Reference come with a browser extension?
Yes. Reference includes a Safari extension today, and a Chrome extension is coming soon.
What can I add to a board?
You can add images, videos, YouTube videos, and GIFs, then use sticky notes for text so every idea, reference, and thought stays together in one place.
Can Reference download YouTube videos?
No. Reference can play YouTube videos inside your workflow, but it does not download them because of YouTube's terms and conditions.
What do the widgets do?
Widgets can surface random inspiration or bring a specific saved project back into view, so the right references stay close at hand.
