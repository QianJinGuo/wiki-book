# ImageToVideoAI - #1 Image to Video AI Generator Online
ImageToVideo.ai is an online tool that converts static images into video content using AI. The platform enables users to upload images and have them animated into video sequences, suitable for social media content creation, marketing materials, and creative projects.
Key features:
1. **Simple Upload Process**: Users can upload JPG, PNG, or WebP images and select from various animation styles.
2. **Multiple Animation Styles**: The tool offers different animation presets including parallax effects, zoom and pan, and character animation for portraits.
3. **Resolution Options**: Outputs at various resolutions suitable for different platform requirements.
4. **Commercial Licensing**: The generated videos come with commercial usage rights, making them suitable for business marketing applications.
5. **Speed**: Processing is cloud-based and designed for quick turnaround, typically completing within minutes.
The article discusses how AI-powered image-to-video generation is democratizing video content creation, enabling individuals and small businesses to produce engaging video content without expensive equipment or technical skills.
