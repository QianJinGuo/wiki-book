## Outbound is hard to learn, execute, and maintain.
The tools are expensive A typical set-up - data enrichment, inbox warming, sequencing - runs over $20,000 a year before you send a single email. Agencies cost even more and optimize for meetings booked, not quality pipeline.
01
The maintenance is constant Sequences break. Tools go offline. Data stops syncing. You spend over half your time on outbound keeping the systems working instead of improving messaging or talking to prospects.
02
You can't tell what's working List sizing, audience segmentation, offer testing, experiment design — most founders are learning these on the job. Without a methodology, every campaign feels like a shot in the dark. 
03

## Frequently asked questions
*   Who is this for? Early-stage founders and growth leaders going 0→1 with outbound, or running outbound but lacking the time, team, or methodology to scale it to hit their goals.    
*   How is this different from an AI BDR? AI BDRs are designed to replace entry-level reps at established sales organizations — companies with a working motion, a defined ICP, and a known playbook, where the goal is volume and the bar for quality is "good enough."
Lightfield Pipeline Generation is for teams at the other end. Your motion isn't established yet. You don't have a playbook to automate. We're a capacity multiplier for the best people on your team, not a replacement for the cheapest ones. We bring methodology and execution capacity so founders and operators can run more experiments, learn faster, and make better decisions about how to scale growth.    
*   How is this different from an outbound agency? Agencies optimize for meetings booked. Their incentive is volume, and the playbook walks out the door when the engagement ends.
Lightfield optimizes for pipeline most likely to convert to revenue. Every data point in your CRM — your closed-won customers, your closed-lost reasons, your call transcripts, your team's connection graph — informs which accounts we target, who we contact, and what we say. The result is fewer meetings with better-fit prospects, not more meetings with worse ones. And because the motion runs on your CRM, the playbook stays with you.    
*   What’s different about running this on your CRM versus a standalone outbound platform? Standalone platforms start cold. They build a TAM from a generic database and your email history, then write sequences against an ICP doc. Lightfield starts with your closed-won customers, your meeting transcripts, and your team’s connection graph. The team scoring your accounts and writing your sequences is operating on the information you share with them, not a comprehensive understanding of every single conversation you’ve ever had with your prospects or customers.    
*   Do I need to use Lightfield’s CRM to access this? Yes. The motion runs on your customer context. If your data isn’t in Lightfield, we can’t build on top of it.    
*   How long until I see results, and what does success look like? Setup takes a few weeks: target list scored, sequences written, mailboxes warmed. Results take 4–6 weeks of live sending. Success isn’t just meetings booked — it’s a clear answer on the hypotheses we set together: which segments to keep targeting, which offers to drop, and what changes to make to your messaging.
